Steps to Connect
  1. Open Google Sheets — use your existing sheet or create a new one.
  2. Set Row 1 headers to: # Customer Name Invoice # Boxes Status Paid Notes
  3. Enter orders from Row 2 onward. Set Status to one of the values above.
  4. Go to File → Share → Publish to web. Choose Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish.
  5. Copy the URL (looks like: …/pub?gid=0&output=csv). Paste it above and click Save & Test.
  6. Open the Warehouse and Pickup links above in the TV browsers. Done!
✓ Daily Use
TV screens auto-refresh every 30 seconds. Update status in Google Sheets on your master computer — changes appear on TV within 30 seconds. At end of day, download your sheet as Excel for records, then clear rows below the header for the next day. No website changes ever needed.
