Steps to Connect
  1. Open Google Sheets and use your existing sheet or create a new one.
  2. Set Row 1 headers: # Customer Name Invoice # Boxes Status Paid Notes
  3. Enter your orders from Row 2 onward. Set Status to one of the values above.
  4. Go to File → Share → Publish to web → Comma-separated values (.csv) → Publish.
  5. Copy the URL. Paste it above and click Save & Test Connection.
  6. Open the Warehouse and Customer Pickup links in the TV browsers. Done!
✓ Daily Use — Screens auto-refresh every 30 seconds. Update status in Google Sheets on your master computer — changes appear on TV within 30 seconds. At end of day, download the sheet as Excel for records, then clear rows for the next morning. No website changes ever needed.
